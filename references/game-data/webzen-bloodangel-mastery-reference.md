> Referencia externa. Revisar compatibilidade com a versao do Blood Moon antes de publicar.

## Descricao

Lorencia Knights worshipping a sword also started to believe in God who can protect themselves in the battlefield where terror rules. They created weapons and armors signifying craving for angels who can protect them in the battlefield where belief in absolute god leading them to victory and terror about death are the only things left. White signifies God's blessing leading the war to victory, and red signifies angel's blood which will protect them. Mercy only means the road to death in the war. Go ahead with angels toward the road to victory stained with red blood.

## Aquisicao

- New equipment item is added for characters who completed 3rd class change over Lv. 400.
- Added 3 types of Mastery Equipment (Normal Item, Excellent Item, Set Item).
